Who are we?

SUNSHINE is a small grassroots user-led community organisation in London, dedicated to spreading joy and community spirit through a range of community based activities and programmes that bring people together, including promoting healthy living and wellbeing, nurturing connections and transforming lives while helping to make a positive impact in our local community.

What we do

At SUNSHINE, we bring people together in community spirit, while spreading joy, nurturing connections and transforming lives. We provide community-based activities, programmes and events in London.
